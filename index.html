Freshly Baked Cheese Cake

We use a flavorful cream cheese imported directly from a long-established factory in Denmark. Every day, we bake our cheesecakes at the kitchens attached to each of our stores, a dozen at a time, round the clock. We also pay great care to the raisins lining the bottom of the cakes.
